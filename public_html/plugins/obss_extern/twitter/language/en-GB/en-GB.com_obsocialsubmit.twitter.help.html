By default, you will have a pair of Consumer Key & Consumer Serect, that's default value and MAY NOT WORK.
Please go to http://foob.la/twkeys to find a working pair for testing purpose.

We RECOMMEND to create a new Twitter app for your own with your branding, please follow this instruction for doing that.

When you have a working pair of Consumer Key & Consumer Secret, please follow this STEP-BY-STEP:

1. Fill Consumer Key & Consumer Secret.
2. Click [Save] (in Joomla 1.7) OR [Apply] (in Joomla 1.5) to save above pair.
3. If you have Access Token & Access Token Secret, fill it and click [Apply] button to Login and DONE. Otherwise, just ignore this step.
4. [Sign in with Twitter] button appears. Click on it to go to Twitter authentication window. Follow the instruction on that window to login into your Twitter account.
5. Welcome back, your Twitter account is now connected to obSocialSubmit. DONE.

Information:
- You can disconnect and re-connect to another Twitter account of yours.
- Turn on DEBUG Mode to get some debug information (if necessary). It's NOT RECOMMENDED.
- If it still doesn't work, feel free to contact us at http://foob.la/support.
